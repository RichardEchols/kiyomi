## Quick Reference: Source Tiers

| Tier | Examples | Default Trust |
|------|----------|---------------|
| 1 - Primary | Official docs, original video, direct quotes | Highest |
| 2 - Wire Services | AP, Reuters, AFP | Very High |
| 3 - Major News | NYT, WSJ, BBC, WaPo | High (note bias) |
| 4 - Specialty | Trade pubs, academic journals | High (for expertise) |
| 5 - Commentary | Substacks, podcasts, opinion | Medium |
| 6 - Social | Individual tweets, posts | Verify independently |
