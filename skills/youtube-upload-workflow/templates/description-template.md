## Tips for Great Descriptions

1. **Front-load keywords** - First 2-3 lines appear in search
2. **Use timestamps** - Improves watch time and UX
3. **Include links** - Drive traffic to your other content
4. **Add hashtags** - 3-5 relevant hashtags at the end
5. **CTA in text** - Ask for likes/comments/subs in description too
6. **Update for evergreen** - Refresh links and info periodically
