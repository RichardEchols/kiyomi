# RE LawManager — Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to RE LawManager

You're set up and ready to go. Here's how to get the most out of your first week.

---

## Day 1: Look Around (5 minutes)

Your firm is already configured. When you log in, you'll find:

1. **Dashboard** — Overview of active cases, upcoming tasks, and recent activity
2. **Cases** — 10 sample PI cases pre-loaded with realistic data. Browse them to see how case tracking works.
3. **Contacts** — Clients, opposing counsel, adjusters, and experts already organized
4. **Billing** — Time entries and cost tracking structured per case
5. **Tasks** — Deadlines and to-dos tied to specific cases

**Action:** Log in and spend 5 minutes clicking through. No setup required.

---

## Day 2-3: Add Your First Real Case

Ready to use it for real? Here's how:

### Create a New Case
1. Click **+ New Case** from the Cases page
2. Fill in: Case name, client, date of incident, case type
3. Add opposing party and counsel info
4. Set the case status (Intake, Active, Discovery, Settlement, etc.)

### Add Contacts
1. Go to **Contacts** and click **+ New Contact**
2. Enter name, role (Client, Attorney, Adjuster, Expert, etc.), phone, email
3. Link them to the relevant case

### Upload Documents
1. Open any case and go to the **Documents** tab
2. Drag and drop files — demand letters, medical records, police reports, anything
3. Files are stored securely and tied to the case

---

## Day 4-5: Track Your Work

### Log Time & Billing
1. Open a case and go to **Billing**
2. Click **+ New Entry** to log time or costs
3. Add description, hours, rate, or flat cost
4. Everything rolls up into case totals automatically

### Manage Tasks
1. Open a case and go to **Tasks**
2. Click **+ New Task** — set a title, due date, and priority
3. Mark tasks complete as you go
4. Dashboard shows upcoming deadlines across all cases
