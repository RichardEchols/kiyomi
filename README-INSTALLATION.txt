Kiyomi Bot v5.0

INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS:
===========================

MacOS (Recommended):
1. Download the DMG from https://kiyomibot.ai
2. Open the DMG and drag Kiyomi to Applications (or double-click to run)
3. Follow the setup wizard in your browser
4. Pick a preset and connect your AI CLI (Claude, Codex, or Gemini)
5. Create a Telegram bot via @BotFather and paste the token

From ZIP (Manual):
1. Unzip and double-click "Install Kiyomi.command"
2. Follow the setup wizard at http://127.0.0.1:8765

WHAT YOU NEED:
- macOS 12+ (Apple Silicon or Intel)
- A Telegram account
- At least one AI CLI installed:
  - Claude CLI (requires Claude Max/Pro subscription, $20/mo)
  - Codex CLI (requires ChatGPT Plus subscription, $20/mo)
  - Gemini CLI (free with a Google account)
